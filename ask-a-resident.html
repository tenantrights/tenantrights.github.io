Spotlight on Continental West Mobilehome Park

Stories

Greg Mason
Manager, ​MHC, Continental, LLC.

    You have come to the website for the Tucson Tenants BLOG. Currently under remodel!! Thanks for your patience, as we redesign its purpose and update the contents.


    Formerly, the site was a host for the Continental West Tenants Association, a group of residents who tried to come together in unity against an abusive Landlord and Apparent Slumlord lead by a wealthy California family, and Greg Mason. 


    Unfortunately the association failed because the owner and managers sabotaged all attempts for the tenants to meet and organize, park manager Erika Villalvazo admitting to tearing down meeting announcements, Newport Pacific's upper management and office manager Carolyn York refusing to allow the association to use the clubhouse for meetings, in violation of State Law, leaving tenant's outdoors in 109° temperatures!


    This site is now a private blog set out to help and inform Mobilehome Park Tenants and Residents.


    In Tucson, Arizona, Greg Mason is a person of record for the Mobilehome Parks, Continental West and Diamond Grove. As this site grows, it will list all past and present mobilehome parks owned by Mr. Mason, and describe the treatment of the residents and their living conditions. It will also identify key members, and some family members, who also control the mobilehome parks. This information, can help residents identify who is the person of last resort if they need to resolve problems. The parks in Tucson are managed by Newport Pacific Capital Corp. 


    If you are considering buying a home in one of Greg's parks, or raising your children in one of his parks, you might want to speak to a tenant of one of his parks first, and get their opinion on living under Greg Mason's lordship and about how his management team treat their tenants, like subjects were once treated.


    Opinions always vary of course.


    One resident says that they want to move out of here but can't find another place to live because MHC Continental, LLC filed for an eviction against them and even though the rent was paid and eviction was cancelled, it now effects their record and their ability to find another home. They feel trapped here and angered that the park would be so brutal and destroy their resident record, even after the matter was resolved to the best of their ability. Smells like Slumlord at Continental West!


    In May 2017, Gas service at Continental West Mobile home park was out for almost a week, because of issues with the system holding pressure. The Corporation Commission refused to allow the gas to be turned on until the park repaired the issues. They felt no need to rush and resident's suffered. Days later, power was disrupted for several tenants. Management claims the outages were beyond their control, many disagree and think that they should have put some urgency into the repairs, but of course that would have cost them big bucks.


    One tenant says that his park, Continental West, is saying his rent will increase over $100 a month over a 3 year period, because the new park management company wants to completely devalue the rent incentives offered to many tenants when they purchased a home here. Is this a "Bait and Switch"?


    Do you have children? Management has failed in controlling speeders that jet through the Continental West Mobilehome park. One tenant moves just because she was concerned for the safety of her child, because she lived on the main street where people speed.


    Management has failed to control the feral cat population and there is pet waste throughout the park.


    The park is generally peaceful, although a resident came home to see the police, in three different cases surrounding different homes, in one case guns were drawn and pointed on a home, for several hours.


    One resident had their bike stolen in broad daylight.


    Want to hear more, ask a tenant before you sign on the dotted line!!!
