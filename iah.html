Martha "Erika" Villalvazo (Manager of Continental West Mobilehome Park) petitioned the Courts for an Injunction Against Harassment against a tenant after Erika removed Tenant Association Notice of Meeting Signs from the Office walls, in violation of Arizona Statutes (A.R.S. 33-1453(H)) and because the tenant argued with Erika about what she had done, later taking his trash to the office for removal, since he pays the park to remove his trash.


Acting terrified on the stand at the hearing, because of the acts noted above, Erika got the court to believe that it was severe and repeated harassment and the court issued the injunction. Now the Tenant cannot go into the office, even if he needs to report a fire or gas leak or anything else, or he will be arrested.


This is only a sample of how these park managers treat their tenants in parks managed by Newport Pacific Capital Corporation!!!


The victim of the Injunction is a tenant in the park who formed the Tenants Association. Erika had been trying to sabotage the tenants association by interfering, removing notices of meetings and the park she manages refused to allow tenants to meet in the clubhouse, also in violation of state law. This is clearly another method of stopping the tenants from organizing a tenants association.
